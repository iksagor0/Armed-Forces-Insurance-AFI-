Helpful Tips

What Is Comprehensive Coverage?

Comprehensive Coverage, also known as "Other Than Collision" Coverage, provides coverage for your vehicle due to damage caused from theft, vandalism, fire, weather, or colliding with an animal.

What Is Collision Coverage?

Collision Coverage pays for damage to your vehicle due to colliding with another vehicle or object other than an animal.
