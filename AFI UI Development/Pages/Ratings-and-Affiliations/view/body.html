AUSA

The Association of the United States Army is a private, non-profit organization that supports the Army - active, National Guard, Reserve, civilians, retirees and family members. Armed Forces Insurance has a strong affiliation with AUSA and is proud to be a corporate member.

 

Demotech, Inc.

Since 1985, Demotech, Inc. has provided responsive services to address actuarial and financial analysis issues, whether the issues have been for a particular insurer or prevalent throughout the insurance industry. Visit Demotech.com to find out more.

NAMIC

The National Association of Mutual Insurance Companies is a trade association comprised of insurance companies that impact the property/casualty insurance industry. As a NAMIC member, AFI has national legislative representation and greater access to resources. Visit NAMIC to find out more.

Property Casualty Insurers Association of America (PCI)

Property Casualty Insurers Association of America is a trade association that represents Armed Forces Insurance and other insurance companies before state and federal lawmakers.

Institute for Business & Home Safety (IBHS)

Armed Forces Insurance looks for ways to help our members reduce losses. One of our sources of information comes from the Institute for Business & Home Safety (IBHS), a non-profit association. IBHS researches, educates and communicates lessons learned from natural disasters.
