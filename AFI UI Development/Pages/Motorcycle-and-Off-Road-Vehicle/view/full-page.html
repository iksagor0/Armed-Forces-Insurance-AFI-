Motorcycle and Off-Road Vehicle Insurance

Whether you're cruising on your bike or off-road trail riding on your 4-wheeler, we will have you and your ride covered. Through our access to a large network of top-rated national carriers, an Armed Forces Insurance agent will work closely with you to find you the best policy and value for your motorcycle and off-road vehicle insurance needs.

Start Your Quote Now

Motorcycle Insurance can protect you from the following:

  • Paying for the repair or replacement of another motorist's vehicle or property, including vehicles, homes, or other structures
  • Paying medical costs, loss of income, and funeral expenses of other people involved in an accident
  • Additional coverage for accessories, such as: saddlebags, backrests, and chrome pieces
  • Damage when it hits, or is hit by another vehicle or object
  • Protection from theft, fire or vandalism

Off-Road Vehicle Insurance

We know you love your off-road vehicle. Make sure you're covered if it is stolen or badly damaged. Proper off-road vehicle insurance coverage can help protect your investment. Off-road vehicle insurance can even provide coverage while your vehicle is in storage or needs a tow.

Many states require off-road vehicle insurance for vehicles operated on state-owned land, and your homeowner policy might not cover you if you ride off your own property. That's why off-road vehicle insurance coverage is especially important.

Off-Road Vehicle Insurance covers all type of off-road vehicles such as:

  • All-Terrain vehicle (ATV)
  • Golf cart
  • Utility vehicle (UTV)
  • Snowmobile
  • Trail bike

Why do I need Off-Road Vehicle Insurance?

Insuring your off-road vehicle can protect you from the following:

  • Damage when it hits, or is hit by another vehicle or object
  • Protection when damages are caused to someone else's property while operating your off-road vehicle
  • Bodily injury protection if certain damages injure or kill someone while operating your off-road vehicle
  • Protection from theft, fire or vandalism

Most off-road vehicle owners don't realize that off-road vehicles have a high theft rate, and your homeowner insurance may not cover your off-road vehicle if you ride it off your property.
