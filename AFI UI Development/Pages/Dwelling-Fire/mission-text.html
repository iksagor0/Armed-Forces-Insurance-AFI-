Dwelling/Fire Insurance

A dwelling fire policy, sometimes called landlord insurance, is similar to a homeowner insurance policy as it protects homes that are rented to others, vacant or occupied by persons other than the owner. Basic coverage insures the dwelling from perils such as fire, wind and hail damage, and vandalism. We can review your individual situation for the policy that best fits your needs. Ask us about options for properties titled in an LLC or Corporation.
