The first step is to find out whether you have a Condominium Association's Master Policy. Based on that management document, we can advise you as to what coverage you need. AFI Condominium Insurance is written for your individual situation, and may include any or all of these coverages:

  • Condo interior
  • Personal property
  • Loss of use
  • Personal liability
  • Medical payments to others
  • Loss assessment

Using a variety of optional endorsements and making sure your condo is insured to current rebuild cost will provide added peace of mind. The type of coverage AFI can offer is determined by:

  • Owner-occupied or rental status
  • Year built
  • Construction type
  • Age of roof
  • Property location
  • Distance to the nearest fire hydrant/fire station
  • Condominium Association's Master Policy

You may be eligible for these money-saving discounts:
