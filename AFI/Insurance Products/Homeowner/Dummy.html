Additional coverages with the military life in mind.

Homeowner insurance isn't a “one size fits all” policy. That's why you have an option to add additional coverages that allow you to customize your policy to your unique needs.

Earthquake coverage
Covers damage and loss caused by an earthquake.
Flood coverage
Homeowner policies exclude flood damage. Get flood coverage to protect yourself from one of the most common natural disasters in the U.S.
Animal liability coverage
Protects you in the event of damages or injuries caused by your dog or other pet that you own.
Identity theft expense coverage
Helps pay for expenses incurred resulting from identity fraud.
Water backup and sump overflow coverage
Covers additional causes of water damage including water that backs up through sewer or drains or overflows or is discharged from a sump pump.
… and many more
